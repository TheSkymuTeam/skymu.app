Skype, reimagined for the modern era. Meet Skymu.

It looks just like the classic program you love, but connects to all the new platforms your friends are on.

Search for other people on Skymu

Skymu connects to multiple popular messaging platforms. Search through a list of everybody connected to Skymu, on any platform, at any given time.

See what you can do with Skymu

Video calling

Skymu supports native video calling through WebRTC. Chat for hours on supported platforms. (Coming soon)

Voice calling

Skymu supports platform-specific voice calling across multiple different plugins. Talk for hours with a friend or a group.

Instant messaging

Whether you have a quick question, want to do a friendly catch-up, or send announcements to hundreds of people, chat using various protocols and platforms. Discord, Matrix, MSNP15, Chat Completions, and XMPP are officially supported.

Skype-era features

Are you feeling more nostalgic than usual? Why not try using one of your Skype database backups with Skymu for a real 2011 experience?

Do even more with Skymu

Call phones

Place and receive calls and manage your contacts with Skymu. All you need is a Bluetooth-capable cell phone to get started.

Join the community

Every day, dedicated users around the world use Skymu to keep in touch for free. Why don't you join them and share your experience using the program?

Fully open-source

The Skymu application, plugins, API, additional compoents, and even this very website are completely open-source, licensed under the AGPLv3. Anyone can contribute to the source code and improve Skymu; yes, even you!
