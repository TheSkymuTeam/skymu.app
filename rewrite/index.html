Skype, reimagined for the modern era. Meet Skymu.

It looks just like the classic program you love, but connects to all the new platforms your friends are on.

Search for other people on Skymu

Skymu connects to more than 20 popular messaging platforms. Search through a list of everybody connected to Skymu, on any platform, at any given time.

See what you can do with Skymu

Video calling

Skymu supports native video calling through WebRTC. Chat for hours on supported platforms. (Coming soon) — See how

Voice calling

Skymu supports both platform-specific voice calling and Bluetooth management of your cell phone. (Bluetooth management coming soon) — Read more

Instant messaging

Quick question or a friendly catch-up, type and chat whenever you need to. Discord, Matrix, MSNP15, and XMPP supported. — See more free features

Skype-era features

Feeling more nostalgic than usual? Why not try reading one of your Skype database backups with Skymu for a real 2011 experience? — Try it now

Do even more with Skymu

Manage your cell phone

Place and receive calls and manage your contacts with Skymu. All you need is a Bluetooth-capable cell phone to get started. — Find out more

Non-messaging platforms

Skymu plans to support platforms not meant for instant messaging, such as Reddit and Bluesky. — Read more
